Jack in the Bx

A coding project

Enter Jack's age, and the size of his Box. For each multiple of the age, the program will display "Jack", and for each multiple of the size of the Box, the program will display "Box".
But here's the real magic! A number that is a multiple of both Jack's age, and the size of the box will display "Jack in the Box!"

So Jack it up!

Note: The results of all three methods will be the same. These options just use different methods to obtain those results.
